What Kisaan Sathi does

Everything a farm advisor would tell you — instantly

Built for how farmers actually communicate. No new app to learn. No internet plan needed beyond basic WhatsApp.

🎙️

Voice-first, dialect-aware

Send a voice note in Bhojpuri, Maithili, or any regional dialect. Kisaan Sathi understands what you say — even on a ₹800 phone.

📷

Photo crop diagnosis

Snap a photo of a sick leaf, pest, or soil patch. AI trained on Indian crop varieties identifies the disease and recommends treatment in plain language.

🌤️

Hyperlocal weather & mandi prices

Get rainfall forecasts for your block and mandi prices for your district — not a generic state average. Your farm, your market.

📜

Government scheme navigator

Tell Kisaan Sathi your land size and crop. It tells you exactly which PM-Kisan, PMFBY, or state schemes you qualify for, and guides you through applying.

💊

Fertiliser & pesticide guide

Get precise dosage, timing, and mixing instructions for inputs — in your language. No more guessing from Hindi-only labels.

👨‍🌾

Escalate to a human

Complex legal or credit questions get routed to a live Krishi Mitra agent. AI handles 90%, humans handle the 10% that matter most.

How it works

Zero friction. Four steps.

Designed so that a first-time smartphone user in a Bihar village can use it without help.

1

Save the number

One WhatsApp contact. No app download, no registration, no OTP. Works on any phone with WhatsApp installed.

2

Ask in any format

Voice note in your dialect, a blurry photo of a leaf, or a typed question in Hindi or English. All accepted, all understood.

3

Get an expert answer in under 10 seconds

Speech-to-text, language detection, crop disease model, scheme database, and mandi price API — all combined into one clear response.

4

Act with confidence

The right pesticide, the right dose, the right time to sell. Proactive alerts reach you before rain, before price drops, before deadlines.
